About ArpyEscrow

Reimagining New York Real Estate — From Contract to Closing

Real Estate Transactions. Done Right, Done Fast.

What Is ArpyEscrow?

ArpyEscrow is a purpose-built transaction management platform designed exclusively for New York residential real estate. It replaces the fragmented patchwork of emails, phone calls, and paper trails that have defined the NY closing process for decades with a single, secure, attorney-grade workspace where every party to a deal — buyers, sellers, attorneys, lenders, and title agents — operates from the same page.

From the moment an offer is accepted through the final closing, ArpyEscrow manages every stage of the transaction lifecycle: contract drafting and negotiation, integrated e-signatures, automated deadline tracking (including the critical 45-day mortgage commitment clock), role-based document exchange, party management, and real-time status updates that keep all stakeholders aligned and accountable.

Who Is It For?

ArpyEscrow serves the professionals and consumers at the center of every New York real estate deal. Purchaser's attorneys and seller's attorneys use the platform to exchange contracts, track rider negotiations, and manage execution workflows. Lenders monitor commitment deadlines and upload approval documentation. Buyers and sellers gain transparent visibility into where their deal stands — without chasing their attorney for a status call. Title companies and real estate agents benefit from centralized access to deal data that eliminates the information silos responsible for missed deadlines and preventable delays.

Who Builds It?

ArpyEscrow is built by attorneys, for attorneys — founded by a New York lawyer with hands-on transactional experience who understands the legal, procedural, and practical demands of closing real property in the state. Every workflow, notification rule, and compliance safeguard in the platform reflects real-world deal knowledge, not a generic SaaS template adapted from another state's closing customs. ArpyEscrow is headquartered on Long Island, New York, and is part of the broader Arpy platform family, which includes ArpyFlow (a geolocation-based dispatch platform for real estate and home service professionals) and ArpyRewards (a referral and commission management system).

🔍 Title Insurance Portal

NY Title Insurance — Managed Inside Your Deal Room

ArpyEscrow is the only New York real estate platform with a built-in title insurance portal. From ordering the title search to issuing the final policy — every step is tracked, automated, and visible to every party in the deal.

Automated from Day 1

Title Search Triggered the Moment Your Contract Executes

The moment a contract is fully executed in ArpyEscrow, the title portal activates automatically. A title search is initiated, deadlines are set, and all parties are notified — without a single manual step.

🏷️

Title Company Selection

The purchaser's attorney selects a title company from ArpyEscrow's directory — or inputs their preferred provider. Underwriter options include First American, Stewart, Fidelity, and Old Republic.

🔎

60-Year Chain of Title Search

Automatically tracks what's being searched: deeds, open mortgages, tax liens, IRS judgments, HPD/DOB violations, UCC filings, easements, and estate proceedings.

📋

ALTA Commitment Parsing (Schedule A & B)

When the commitment arrives, ArpyEscrow classifies every exception: standard items are auto-approved; non-standard exceptions (open liens, violations, judgments) are flagged as "must cure" and trigger attorney tasks.

Exception Clearance

Every Title Problem Has an Automated Resolution Path

ArpyEscrow manages the most common NY title exceptions automatically — assigning tasks, sending reminders, and tracking resolution so nothing falls through the cracks before closing.

🏦

Open Mortgage & Lien Clearance

Auto-assigns "Obtain payoff letter" task to seller's attorney. Sends follow-up every 5 days until the lien is cleared and uploaded.

🏙️

HPD / DOB Violations (NYC)

Detects open NYC violations and routes them to the seller's attorney — either to cure the violation or obtain a title insurance endorsement over it.

📐

Survey Exceptions

Prompts buyer to order an updated survey or execute a survey waiver — tracks the response and marks the exception cleared automatically upon upload.

⚖️

Estate / Probate Issues

Flags estate proceedings as a hard stop, alerts both attorneys, and tracks resolution through Letters Testamentary or probate court orders.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ArpyEscrow.

ArpyEscrow is a digital real estate transaction management platform that connects buyers, sellers, attorneys, and lenders in one place. Instead of managing a deal over emails, phone calls, and PDFs, every party logs in, sees their tasks, and moves the deal forward together — from first offer to final close.
ArpyEscrow is designed for New York home buyers, home sellers, real estate attorneys (both seller's and purchaser's), and mortgage lenders and loan officers. Every role has a dedicated portal tailored to their specific responsibilities in a transaction.
New York real estate transactions require both a seller's attorney and a purchaser's attorney. Each gets their own dedicated portal on ArpyEscrow. The seller's attorney uploads the initial contract; the purchaser's attorney uploads redlines. Each version is tracked. Once negotiation is finalized, the purchaser's attorney sends it to the buyer for e-signature via Dropbox Sign. When signed, the contract is automatically marked "fully executed" and the 45-day mortgage commitment clock starts.
Yes. ArpyEscrow uses Dropbox Sign (formerly HelloSign) for all electronic signatures. These are legally binding under the ESIGN Act and UETA. Every signed document is stored securely and generates an audit trail.
In New York, buyers typically have 45 days from contract execution to secure a mortgage commitment from their lender. This is a critical deadline — missing it can allow the seller to cancel the contract and keep the down payment. ArpyEscrow starts tracking this clock automatically the moment a contract is fully executed and sends warnings at Day 38 if no commitment letter has been uploaded.
Buyers fill out their financial profile once — income, employment, credit score range, down payment, and desired loan type. ArpyEscrow submits this to multiple NY lenders simultaneously. Lenders review the profile and return pre-approval letters, which are stored directly in the buyer's portal for use with any offer.
ArpyEscrow is currently focused on New York real estate transactions, with integrations built specifically for the NY Bar attorney directory, SONYMA lenders, and New York's two-attorney closing model. Expansion to other states is planned for the future.
It's simple — search the verified NY Bar directory within ArpyEscrow, find your attorney (or search by name), and send them a direct invite from the platform. They'll receive a branded email with a secure link to accept and join your transaction. No tech setup required on their end.
Yes — ArpyEscrow includes a full NY Title Insurance Portal that activates automatically when a contract is executed. It manages the entire title process: ordering the search, receiving the ALTA commitment (Schedule A and B), classifying exceptions, tracking clearance of open mortgages, liens, HPD/DOB violations, survey issues, and estate proceedings, calculating TIRSA-based premiums, and tracking through to final policy issuance. Supported underwriters include First American, Stewart, Fidelity, and Old Republic.
ArpyEscrow tracks and routes the most common NY title exceptions: open mortgages (assigns payoff letter task to seller's attorney), tax liens and judgments, HPD and DOB violations for NYC properties, survey exceptions (prompts buyer to sign waiver or order new survey), mechanic's liens, UCC filings, and estate/probate issues. Each type has its own automated resolution workflow with task assignments and follow-up reminders.
ArpyFlow is Arpy Inc.'s on-demand home services platform for Long Island and New York homeowners — connecting them with vetted handymen, plumbers, landscapers, cleaners, and more, available 24/7. When an ArpyEscrow deal closes, new homeowners automatically receive access to ArpyFlow for move-in services. Sellers closing their home can book move-out cleaning directly. Both apps are built by Arpy Inc. and are designed to serve every stage of the New York real estate experience.
A title rundown (or title update) is a final title search performed 24–48 hours before closing to confirm no new liens, judgments, or encumbrances have been filed since the original title commitment. ArpyEscrow automatically alerts the title agent 2 days before the scheduled closing date to order the rundown — and flags both attorneys immediately if any new issues are found.

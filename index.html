Why ArpyEscrow

Every feature is built for the way New York deals actually close.

Contract Management

Upload, redline, and negotiate contracts between attorneys with full version tracking. Every draft is timestamped and auditable.

Integrated E-Signatures

Buyers sign directly on the platform — legally binding, timestamped, and audit-trailed. No printing, scanning, or mailing.

Automated Deadlines

The 45-day mortgage commitment clock starts automatically when the contract is executed. Reminders go to every party — no manual tracking.

Role-Based Access

Each party — buyer, seller, attorney, lender, title — sees only what they should. Structured permissions designed by attorneys who understand confidentiality.

Party Management

Replace an attorney mid-deal, add a lender, swap a title company — ArpyEscrow handles transitions cleanly with preserved history and automatic notifications.

Built for New York

How It Works

1

Create a Deal

The seller's attorney creates a new deal on the platform and invites the purchaser's attorney. Both sides are in the same workspace from day one.

2

Negotiate the Contract

Upload the initial contract, exchange redlines and riders, and negotiate terms — all tracked with timestamps and version history.

3

Execute and Track

The buyer signs via integrated e-signatures. The moment the contract is fully executed, ArpyEscrow starts the mortgage commitment clock and keeps every party on track through closing.
